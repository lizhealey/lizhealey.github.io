Research Focus Areas

I work on machine learning for healthcare with a focus on developing methods that enhance clinical decision-making and improve patient outcomes. My research bridges the gap between AI methods and real-world healthcare applications.

  1. Apply machine learning to improve the understanding and treatment of diabetes, particularly leveraging continuous glucose monitoring
  2. Develop machine learning methods that leverage observational data to enhance clinical decision making
  3. Translate machine learning research into provider-facing and patient-facing tools to enhance patient care and outcomes
